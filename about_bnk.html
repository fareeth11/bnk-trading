About Us

BNK General trading llc is a young and vibrant company based in Ajman, uae. We have a strong commitment to bridge markets and making quality products & brands accessible at competitive prices. Our motto is to operate ethically, efficiently and reliably.

It is managed by Mr.Sapnil Sharma, a young B.tech / MBA educated professional. He has a strong foundation in UAE and India. His logistics background is an asset for the company.

About Us

BNK General Trading LLC is a young and vibrant trading house based in Ajman, UAE. We have a strong commitment to bridge Markets and making quality products accessible across borders. Established with a Global Vision, BNK leverages its deep-rooted expertise in International Trade to connect businesses with the Goods they need—Efficiently, Ethically, and Reliably.

The company is the brainchild of Mr. Sapnil Sharma, a dynamic entrepreneur with roots in India and a strong foundation in the UAE. His cross-cultural understanding and passion for Global Trade laid the foundation of BNK, with a single-minded mission: to simplify Sourcing and Supply Chains through Trust, Transparency, and Timely Delivery.

Our Vision

To become a trusted global gateway for seamless import and export of High-Quality Goods, and interacting with Businesses by ensuring access to the Right Products at the right time.

Our Mission

• To provide end-to-end Import / Export solutions tailored to the needs of businesses across sectors.
• To maintain the Highest Standards of Ethics, Professionalism, and Service Excellence in every Transaction.
• To build long-term relationships through Integrity, Consistency, and Mutual Growth.
